Rationale for Amendment

While existing statutes extend benefits to select groups, the majority of government employees and executives remain excluded from automatic pension increases. This proposal advocates for inclusivity, ensuring that all retirees receive equitable treatment. The amendment would:

  • Promote Fairness: Address disparities in retirement benefits across government sectors.
  • Ensure Recognition: Honor the contributions of all government employees, regardless of rank or position.
  • Strengthen Cohesion: Foster a unified and equitable retirement system.
  • Encourage Motivation: Inspire continued dedication and service among current government personnel.

Call to Action

This initiative does not oppose the benefits already granted under R.A. 9946, 11059, and 10071. Rather, it seeks to extend similar provisions to all government retirees, thereby creating a more balanced and equitable retirement framework.

We call on our colleagues, stakeholders, and the broader community to support this proposal by signing the campaign and casting a vote in favor of its adoption.
